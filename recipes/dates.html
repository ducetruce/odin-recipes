Stuffed Dates Recipe

A delicious stuffed date, perfect for Ramadan

Ramadan is a time when all Muslims fast and celebrate together. During Ramadan, we often eat these stuffed dates to break the fast. They are easy to make and a delicious treat.

    Ingredients
  • 20 pitted dates
  • 20 blanched almonds, toasted
  • 2 tablespoons chopped toasted almonds, to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) container of creme fraiche
    Instructions
  1. Stuff each date with 1 whole toasted almond.
  2. Arrange on a serving platter.
  3. Spoon a little bit of creme fraiche over each date.
  4. Sprinkle with chopped almonds.
