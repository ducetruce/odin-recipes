April Fool's Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

A funny recipe to try and trick your friends!

This delicious 'sandwich' is perfect for April Fool's Day, Halloween, or even birthday parties. Definitely DO try this at home!

    Ingredients
  • 1 pound cake, cut into ½ slices
  • 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1 cup white frosting
  • ½ teaspoon yellow food coloring, or as need
  • ⅛ teaspoon red food coloring, as needed
    Instructions
  1. Spread butter on both sides of each pound cake slice.
  2. Heat a griddle over medium heat.
  3. Cook cake slices on hot griddle, turning once, until golden brown on each side 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Transfer cake slices to a wire rack to cool completely
  5. Stir frosting, yellow food coloring, and red food coloring together in a bowl until frosting is the color of American cheese
  6. Spread frosting on 1 side of half of the pound cake slices and top with another slice of pound cake
